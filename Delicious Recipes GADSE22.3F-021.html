Recipe for flatbreads:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the following dry ingredients:

    2. - 2 cups all-purpose flour
    - 1 teaspoon baking powder
    - 1/2 teaspoon salt

  2. Add the following wet ingredient to the bowl:

    3. - 1 cup plain yoghurt (or Greek yoghurt)

  3. Stir the mixture until it starts to come together and forms a dough.

  4. Transfer the dough onto a floured surface.

  5. Knead the dough for approximately 5 minutes, using your hands, until it becomes smooth and elastic.

  6. Divide the dough into smaller balls, each about the size of a golf ball.

  7. Using a rolling pin, roll out each ball into a flat disc. Aim for a thickness of around 1/8 to 1/4 inch.

  8. Heat a skillet or non-stick frying pan over medium-high heat.

  9. Place one or two flatbreads onto the heated skillet and cook for about 2 minutes on each side, or until they develop golden-brown spots and puff up slightly.

  10. Serve the freshly cooked flatbreads warm and enjoy them with your favorite dips, curries, or fillings.
